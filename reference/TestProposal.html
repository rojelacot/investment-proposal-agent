How it works: A 130/30 strategy holds 130% long exposure and 30% short exposure within a net 100% invested portfolio. The short book is actively harvested for tax losses. Those losses are "banked" and used to offset the capital gains generated each year when Daniel sells NVIDIA shares through the staged sales program — dramatically reducing the net tax bill.

Options Collar Strategy
Define the downside. Preserve defined upside. No forced sale.
Applied to retained core
$31.5M
NVDA retained after CRT
How it works: After funding the CRT, Daniel retains approximately $31.5M of NVIDIA. An options collar is placed on this position: Daniel buys a protective put (setting a floor below current price — limiting maximum loss) and simultaneously sells a covered call (capping upside above current price — generating premium income). The put premium is partially or fully funded by the call sale, making the strategy low- or zero-cost.
